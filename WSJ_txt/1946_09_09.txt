Abreast of the Market
With widespread and insistent liquidation the three averages hit new 1946 low* yesterday with the industrial average ending at 1*72.03 off *7.93. Undoubtedly the gloomy labor situation was a factor in further depressing an already bearish feeling. Most stocks closed around the lows. There were only fractional and brief rallies during the day and trading died down considerably thereon making the technical picture dismal. Volume of 2.830.000 shares compared with 1.670.000 shares o: Friday.
