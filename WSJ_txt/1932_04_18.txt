MARKET LOWER
Bank Stocks Decline in Dull Trading— Insurance Quiet
The initial session of the new week found little activity in the unlisted securities market. Stocks were generally lower at the opening and downward throughout the period, closing with small losses on the day. In the bank and trust company group, Guaranty was off 7; Central Hanover lost 4, and First National declined 15. Other losses ranged from fractions to 2 points.
